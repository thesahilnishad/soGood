Our Story

Where two traditions
become one table

"Food is memory, and memory is culture. We cook to connect people across borders."

So Good was born from a simple conviction: that Cantonese and Japanese cuisine — both rooted in a reverence for quality ingredients, seasonal rhythm and generational craft — belong together at the same table.

Head Chef Wei Chen trained under masters in Guangzhou for a decade before apprenticing at an Osaka kappo restaurant. That rare dual fluency is what you taste in every dish we serve.

We source daily from BC's finest seafood suppliers, and our produce arrives fresh each morning. No freezers for proteins. No shortcuts for broth. Just two great culinary traditions, cooked with care.
